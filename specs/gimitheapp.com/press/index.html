  • Footballer Philip Haglund wants Veckopengen to teach kids about money

    29 June

    The idea about Gimi started to grow when Haglund started researching about kids and the way they were using their cell phones. Haglund says it was clear that teenagers in Sweden use the Internet on their phones more than any other country, but there was no tool that could handhold or teach them about the use of money and financial goals. Haglund wanted Gimi to be that tool.
